Meet Michael Edson

Smithsonian's Web and New Media Strategy Director




“Can we put the tools of knowledge creation into more hands to catalyze research and discovery? Can we share the joy and meaning of artistic and cultural exploration with more citizens?” (Edson, 2013)

Michael Edson is the innovator behind the Smithsonian Commons project – an effort to make “Smithsonian digital resources free and unrestricted sharing of Smithsonian resources and encouraging new kinds of learning and creation through interaction with Smithsonian research, collections, and communities.” (Smithsonian 2010) The Smithsonian Commons project started in 2009 and its interactive and customizable design provides an individual experience for all Smithsonian visitors.
